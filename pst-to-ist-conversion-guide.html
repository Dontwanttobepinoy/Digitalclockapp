PST to IST Converter: Complete Guide for US West Coast to India

Published: December 17, 2024 Category: Time Zone Conversion Reading Time: 7 minutes

Converting between Pacific Standard Time (PST) and Indian Standard Time (IST) is crucial for business communications between the US West Coast and India. With a 13.5-hour difference, this is one of the largest time zone gaps for international business.

PST to IST Time Difference

Pacific Standard Time (PST) is UTC-8, while Indian Standard Time (IST) is UTC+5:30. This creates a 13.5-hour difference between the two time zones.

Method 2: Manual Calculation

To manually convert PST to IST:

  • Add 13 hours and 30 minutes to PST time
  • If the result is 24:00 or later, subtract 24 hours and add 1 day
  • Example: 9:00 AM PST + 13:30 = 10:30 PM IST

Method 3: Using UTC as Reference

Since PST is UTC-8 and IST is UTC+5:30:

  • Convert PST to UTC by adding 8 hours
  • Convert UTC to IST by adding 5 hours and 30 minutes
  • Total: Add 13 hours and 30 minutes

Daylight Saving Time Considerations

Daylight Saving Time (DST) affects PST to IST conversion during certain periods:

Pacific Daylight Time (PDT)

During DST (March to November), PST becomes PDT (UTC-7):

  • PDT to IST difference: 12.5 hours (instead of 13.5)
  • Example: 9:00 AM PDT = 9:30 PM IST

Pacific Standard Time (PST)

During standard time (November to March):

  • PST to IST difference: 13.5 hours
  • Example: 9:00 AM PST = 10:30 PM IST
